LASAGNA

This incredible Lasagna recipe will change your life!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or Italian Sausage)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara tomato sauce
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, fresh basil

STEPS

  1. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion in a pan
  2. Stir in the marinara sauce and let simmer
  3. Mix the ricotta cheese with an egg and seasoning
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta texture in a baking dish
  5. Top with Mozzarella and Parmesan, then bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes
