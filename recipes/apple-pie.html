APPLE PIE

This Apple Pie is so incredibly it will change your entire life!

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Lemons
  • Pie Crust
  • Sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cut apples into pieces
  2. Place apple slices into pie crust
  3. Sprinkle sugar on them
  4. Squeeze lemons on it
  5. Bake at 450 degrees for 90 minutes straight
